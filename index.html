Who We Are

Crafted with
Local Soul

We are passionate about showing travelers the best of Medan, Lake Toba, and Berastagi with curated, hassle-free itineraries. Born from a love for North Sumatra's landscapes, culture, and cuisine — every journey we craft carries the warmth of local hospitality and the depth of Batak heritage.

Our guides were born and raised here. They know the hidden viewpoints above Samosir, the best warung on the Toba shoreline, the stories behind Maimun Palace that no guidebook mentions.
